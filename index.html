Going Camping

You are about to leave on a camping trip in the mountains, however, you forgot to pack three of the ten essentials and you need to get your house ready before your friends arrive to pick you up in 10 minutes.

Objective

You must pack the three remaining ten essentials in your backpack, close the windows, turn off all the lights, feed the cat, and take the garbage out before you can exit the porch to meet your friends.

Description

Navigate through the house to find which rooms have open windows to close and lights on that should be turned off. Find your cat Tiger and feed her, as well as take out the garbage. Three of the rooms contain a locked cabinet that your gear is stored in. You must choose the correct three items to be able to leave with your friends. You'll have to figure out the combination every time you want to open the cabinet. When you think you have the house prepared and the three missing items packed in your backpack, try to meet your friends. If you are correct you will successfully meet your friends, otherwise you'll have to go back and choose the correct item(s) or complete the unfinished house task(s). You are only allowed three items in your backpack at a time from the ten that are stored in the cabinets. Be on the lookout for clues along the the way!

Directions

Navigate throughout the house and messages will appear as you enter the rooms. If you'd like to take action - hit the button! You may only reach the porch from the entry. If you want to meet your friends, click the meet friends button when you are on the porch. When you are ready to start, click the start game button. Good luck!

Hints

Five rooms have clues as to the items already in your pack, leaving five of the 10 essentials as the potential missing items. The items are randomly stored in each cabinet so the three missing items could all be in one cabinet, could be stored between two cabinets, or there could be one missing item in each cabinet. When you try to meet your friends, you'll learn how many of the items in your pack are correct.
